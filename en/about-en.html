About Us

How we work: what are the criteria, how we verify information, and how you can help us stay updated.

The site was built to help find products and shops that align with the values of compassion. We conduct research and testing to ensure that only brands that are 100% vegan and not tested on animals appear here.

What You Will Find Here

  • Product database with search and filtering
  • Quick ingredient checker
  • Deals board and shopping dates

How Do We Check Brands?

  • Company Policy – Official statements, policy documents, and questionnaires.
  • Certifications – PETA / Leaping Bunny when available.
  • Regulatory Status – Whether there is sales in countries with testing requirements.
  • Supply Chain – Raw materials, suppliers, and subcontractors (as far as can be verified).

The information is based on public sources and information provided by the brands. If you found an update – we would be happy to check.

How Can You Help?

  • Submit a brand that is missing from the database (link + photo/information).
  • Report a change in policy or certification.
  • Share so we can reach more people and companies.

Write to us via the "Contact Us" page – and we will add/update accordingly.
