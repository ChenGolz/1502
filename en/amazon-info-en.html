Shipping, taxes, settings, and fees when buying from Amazon US and Amazon UK to Israel

On this page, we have gathered important information about shipping, taxes, language and currency settings, and tips for saving costs when you buy from Amazon US and Amazon UK.

Important: The information on this page is general only. Prices, shipping terms, and fees change constantly. Always check the final price and the breakdown of taxes/fees on the Amazon checkout page before ordering.
Briefly about the sites we use
Amazon US Amazon UK

For most products on our recommendation pages, you will find links to Amazon US or Amazon UK. Information about the shipping region (US / UK) also appears on the product card.

Amazon US

On Amazon US, many products are marked as: "FREE delivery to Israel on eligible orders over $49".

  • To reach the minimum of $49 for free shipping, you can and should assemble a cart from different brands and sellers – as long as it says next to each product that it is included in free shipping over $49.
  • Average shipping time: Usually around two weeks to Israel (sometimes more or less, depending on the product and the period).
  • Pay attention to Import Fees / Import Charges appearing at checkout – they are part of the final cost.

Amazon UK

  • Average shipping time: Usually about a week, and sometimes even less.
  • On Amazon UK, generally, the larger the basket (more products together in the same order), the average shipping cost per product decreases.
  • Here too, it is important to check the checkout page for the final shipping cost and the amount of Import Charges (if any).
Language and Currency Settings

How to change language and currency on Amazon (US & UK)

Changing site language (Desktop)
  • Log in to Amazon.com or Amazon.co.uk in your account.
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page and look for a flag / globe icon (Language / Country & Region).
  • Click on it and select your preferred language (usually English). If a Hebrew option exists, it can also be selected.
Changing currency to Shekels (ILS) – Desktop Site
  • On the main page, scroll to the bottom of the page and look for a link like Change currency / ₪ ILS (usually next to the region settings).
  • Select ILS – Israeli Shekel and save.
  • In some cases, you will also find it through the menu: Accounts & Lists → Your Account → Ordering and shopping preferences → Change currency.

Even after changing the currency to Shekels, the final charge is made according to the exchange rates and clearing fees of the credit card company / Amazon – therefore it is always advisable to check the final amount in Shekels.

Changing language and currency in the Amazon app
  • Open the app → click on the menu button (≡).
  • Go to Settings.
  • Select Country & Language and change the language and region (e.g., Israel / United States / United Kingdom).
  • In some versions, you can also select the Currency and choose ILS, and if not – prices will be displayed in Dollars / Pounds.
Taxes in Israel

Taxes, VAT, and Import Charges to Israel

  • As of today, for most orders up to approximately $150 (including shipping, excluding insurance), it is possible to avoid additional taxes, but: Always important to read the explanation that appears on Amazon regarding Import Fees Deposit / Import Charges.
  • Above this amount, VAT / additional taxes may apply – Amazon sometimes collects a tax deposit (Import Fees Deposit) in advance and handles customs clearance for you.
  • Before confirming the order, check: Product price + Shipping + Import Charges = Final Price.
Product Variations / Changes

Changes in products and prices

On the product pages on our site, we present estimated price ranges and information correct as of the last update date. On Amazon itself, information can change: price, volume, formula, packaging, shipping terms, and more.

  • Always re-read the full product name and the details / description on the Amazon page.
  • Check the package size (ml / gr / fl oz) and match it to your expectations.
  • If something looks different from what is written here – rely on what appears on the Amazon site at the time of purchase.

Savings Tips

Tips for saving on shipping

  • Try to concentrate as many products as possible in one order – especially on Amazon UK, where the average shipping cost per product decreases as the order amount increases.
  • Check if the same product exists in both US and UK – sometimes the difference in shipping or taxes makes the difference.
  • Compare several products from the same brand to reach a cart above the free shipping amount (e.g., above $49) without buying "just because".
