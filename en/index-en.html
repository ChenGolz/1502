Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Not Tested on Animals" mean?

It means the product and its ingredients were not tested on animals. Here you will only find brands that are both 100% Vegan and Cruelty-Free.

Why is "100% Vegan" important too?

"Cruelty-free" refers to testing. "100% Vegan" refers to ingredients. On this site, we double-filter ‚Äî only what meets both conditions enters the database.
