Why are many Israeli brands Vegan but without PETA / Leaping Bunny?

In Israel, there are many small-to-medium businesses that develop vegan formulas (no animal-derived ingredients) but do not always join international certification programs.

  • Cost and Process ‚Äì Certification requires time, documentation, and sometimes fees.
  • Local Target Audience ‚Äì A brand that sells mainly in Israel sometimes settles for internal policies and transparency.
  • Markets and Regulation ‚Äì If the brand does not sell in countries with animal testing requirements, it may choose not to register for an international program.
