How Do We Check?

Transparency = Trust. Here you can find out how we verify information, what gets added to the site — and what doesn't.

The site only lists brands and products that are 100% Vegan and Not Tested on Animals. If there is any doubt — we prefer not to display it.

How We Verify

  • Official Brand Statement (official website / customer service).
  • Third-party Databases (e.g., PETA / Leaping Bunny / recognized lists) — as a cross-check, not a replacement.
  • Ingredient Check when necessary (to identify non-vegan ingredients).
  • Periodic Audits — statuses change, so we come back to check.

What Does Not Enter the Site

  • Brands without clear/updated information.
  • Brands that are "cruelty-free" but not completely vegan.
  • Products with animal-derived ingredients, even if the brand is partially vegan.
  • Products with inconsistent information between sources.

When Was It Last Updated?

Statuses change. Therefore, a "Last Updated" date appears on brand pages (where available).
